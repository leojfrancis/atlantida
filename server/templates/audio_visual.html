Audio visual - services

There has been a sea level change in the audio visual (AV) ecosystem in the past 10 years. Today’s offerings provide AV over IP, where AV components can receive and send data across the network while offering handy control using almost any handheld device or desktop computer. If there is still no know-how inside your organization when it comes to using AV over IP, NCS is the helper of choice. Our specialty is designing a more conventional AV solution that uses industry standards regarding components and cabling. Our expertise in structured cabling goes hand in hand with the AV integration services we provide, helping you extend systems already in place, create novel ones and maintain both, supporting you for the entire lifespan of whichever choice you make.

Forward-Thinking Solutions for Your Projects Needs

Audio Visual Division at NCS can cooperate with owners/reps, architects and consultants to establish an AV installation and configuration that matches your exact specifications. If you still have doubts, we can furnish you with a comprehensive AV installation design, complete with documentation, to meet your budget limits.
